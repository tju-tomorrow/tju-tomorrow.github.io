My Project

发表于 2025-12-26 阅读次数：

HuggingFace is very generous and really make my project crazy,I think this is my best project
It’s a combination of creativity and a lot of reflections about how I used to do wrong

So this project is really about deeply combining AI with your Notion notes to help you actually absorb the knowledge in them, as a way to fight against how easily we forget things.

Because I don’t think taking notes guarantees that you truly understand or internalize the content. I’ve been using Notion for a long time and have accumulated a lot of notes, but I’ve never really practiced active learning. I’m always just passively browsing old notes.
I am excited to make this open-source in some days
